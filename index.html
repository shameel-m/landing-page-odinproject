Some Fun Facts About Diego

Against England in 1986 World-Cup quarter-final Maradona scored what is widely considered as the best goal of all time.

Maradona made debut for Argentinos Juniors just 10 days before his 16th birthday.

Maradona single-handedly led Argentina to glory in 1986 FIFa World Cup

In 2000, Maradona was awarded the title of FIFA Player of the Century alongside Pelé.
